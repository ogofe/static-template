Frequently asked questions

Do you provide financial assistance?

Yes. Together with forwarding your legal documents, we can help you handle taxes, banking, cheques and payrolls so you can focus on what matters to your business.

Are your Agent Trustworthy?

All of our agents are legal US based citizens, and they go through our screening and vetting process, you can be rest assured your documents and funds are in good hands.

What is the wait period?

We provide assistance on the fly! However, for designated agents, it may take up to a week to provide you with an agent.

Frequently asked questions

How will I get my documents?

Your documents and files will be scanned and emailed to you. However, if you require the original copies, they can be airmailed to your postal address for a small fee.

What is the benefit of a Registered Agent?

A registered agent is required by the US government for businesses incorporated in the US from another country. WorkStar provides you with vetted Agents, Assistants, Tax Accountants and Lawyers. We're here to help you grow your business and scale easily. Leave the paperwork to us.

Can i have feedback on the fly?

Yes! You get notified by email and Slack when anything happens, so you're always updated, and we provide support when you need it.
